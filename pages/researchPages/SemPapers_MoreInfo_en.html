Wiki for Research Group

Case:
The achievements of research groups and their team spirit are based on common scientific interests and collective expertise in a field of research. It is a great challenge to keep an eye on the state of knowledge.

Solution:
The members of the research group document the key data on scientific papers in the field of research in a common semantic Wiki based on OntoWiki. The knowledge scheme is based on criteria for a qualitative literature analysis.

  • Knowledge schema
  • Semi-automatic population process
  • GitHub reository for population process

Next steps & cooperations:
The Wiki is used by the members of the research group BMaKE within the monthly seminars. There is a cooperation with the project "Open Research Knowledge Graph" at the TIB of the University of Hannover.
