Video Platform fbwTube

Case:
The video lectures produced in the university's own recording studio are made available to students in Moodle via thematically appropriate links. However, this means that they are only accessible to registered students and not to the general public.

Solution:
Using a web app, all produced video lectures can be searched online and used flexibly, i.e. across all modules. Intelligent search and filter functions support the search. Data on new videos is entered via a semantic Wiki.

  • Knowledge schema
  • Systems architecture
  • Project resources on GitHub

Next steps & cooperations:
The platform is to be available in several languages by the end of 2019. Furthermore, an extension of the search and filter functions is planned. The project is funded by the THB project "Diversity in Study and Teaching" (EFRE). The platform is not only used at the THB, but also at the cooperating universities: Gdansk University of Technologies, Alfred Nobel University Dnipro, German-Kazakh University Almaty and University of Economics Katowice.
